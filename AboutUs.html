about us

HASAN BUILDCON was established in the year 2012. We have been recognized as one of the top-notch manufacturers and suppliers of Construction and Building Material. Our comprehensive assortment consists of Hollow blocks. These products are made out of Mangalore clay. Building companies are constantly looking at ways

in which they can improve their environmental performance, reduce costs and make their processes more efficient. HASAN BUILDCON can help them achieve these, with their products produced with clay in an environmentally responsible manner delivering considerable benefits to the building industry.

 

HASAN BUILDCON uses Mangalore clay in the manufacture of high quality building materials which decreases the environmental impact of building materials that meet the public demand for greener building materials. Our firm is equipped with a contemporary infrastructure that aids us in the dexterous execution of all the business operations. The varied business activities are skillfully accomplished by our team of diligent professionals. Owing to perfect blend of our infrastructure, and team of professionals, with the right quality of clay we have established a broad client-base across the globe.

VISION
our vision is to revolutionize the construction industry by redefining the way bricks are perceived and utilized. We envision a future where our bricks become synonymous with innovation, sustainability, and architectural excellence.
AIM
de aim to be at the forefront of advancements in brick manufacturing, continuously seeking new methods and technologies to enhance the quality, durability, and environmental impact of our products. Our vision extends beyond traditional brick offerings, as we explore and introduce cutting-edge designs and materials that push the boundaries of what bricks can achieve.
MISSION
Our mission is to empower builders, architects, and homeowners with high-quality bricks that not only meet their functional needs but also exceed their expectations in terms of durability and aesthetic appeal. We believe that a strong foundation is the key to any successful construction project, and that's why we prioritize offering bricks that are known for their strength and reliability.
Sustainability
Sustainability is a core pillar of our vision. We strive to minimize our ecological footprint by embracing eco-friendly practices throughout our operations. From responsibly sourcing raw materials to adopting energy-efficient production processes, we are committed to preserving the environment for future generations.
